InterChristian Leaders 2030

InterChristian Institutefor Leadership Development

InterChristian International is focused on producing more and more second line Christian Leaders irrespective of denominational background. We have a goal of training 2030 second line leaders by the year 2030. Investing in training future leaders means we are putting our hope in action, the hope of our faith, our mission to be carried on to the next generation. This is done by InterChristian Institute for Leadership Development. These Institutes are ideal for young Pastors, Bible seminary fresher, lay persons, pastors spouses, Aspirants who are pondering future ministry and those lay people interested in conducting small group, bible study, Sunday school ministry, youth ministry or Women's fellowship. Students are welcome from any part of the World.
