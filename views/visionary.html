InterChristian Visionary

Our dream will not die if we envision and make it a mission.

Our Current Dream Team includes the CEO Rev. Paul Rajan

Visionary | Pastor | Pastoral Educator | Pastoral Clinician | Entrepreneur | Counselor | Missionary | Author | Journalist | Convention Speaker | Interfaith Leader | Publisher | Radio Program Producer

The Rev. Paul Rajan was born in Tamil Nadu State of South India. His father was an Anglican Christian and mother was a Hindu. He experienced hardship and rejection in his childhood due to the loss of his mother at the age of 7. He committed his life to God and to service of the kingdom of God during his life changing conversion experience at a suicide point while he was 14 years old. He is an ordained minister with Anglican Church of South India for Priestly ministry and Church of South India for Missional engagement. His nationality is Indian New Zealander. He lives in Princeton, New Jersey with his wife Sudha, 8 years old Son Jason and 2 years old son Jabez. Immediately after his theological education in Chennai in India in 1986, he was instrumental in starting a Tamil and a Kannada Bible School in Bangalore India. Then he worked with Trans World Radio and produced gospel program. His radio program took him to hundreds of pulpits of Bible believing Churches and Fellowships and he became a seasoned convention speaker. He started to preach in first convention in the year 1984 from Kallan Kuli Church in Kanya Kumari Dt the tip of India. His recent convention included Christ Church of South India in Toronto, and CSI CT in USA.

He pioneered Christian Life Service as a Teaching and reaching missionary movement in Karnataka state in 1993 and planted 55 worshiping groups and couple of schools. As a passionate leader and a visionary he pioneered Global Peace Mission in New Zealand in 2000 and served the migrant community. He started Interfaith Initiatives in USA in 2008 and brought nearly hundred students to study in USA while he worked as a Dean of Students in an Interfaith Seminary USA where he came to do his masters and doctorate. He was the Pastor of Trinity Church of South India New Jersey and Christ Church of South India in New York together near to four years. Paul was trained in Christian Counseling by CCS India, AU New Zealand and CWR UK, and has rich experience in counseling both Western and Asian Couple and individuals. With his four units of Clinical Pastoral Education and four units of supervisory education in ACPE USA, he is an excellent Pastoral Educator and Chaplain.

Paul has Masters in Divinity from Interfaith Seminary NY, Masters in English Literature from Madurai University India, completes his PhD in Education, Counseling and Supervision from USA. He is the recipient of Honorary Doctor of Divinity from Bethania Theological College & Seminary, India. He has translated several books from English to Tamil and Kannada. He is the editor and publisher of tabloid newspaper Global Peace Messenger in New Zealand and InterChristian Times in USA. He has broadcasted thousands of gospel and social radio broadcasts. Some of them includes Secrets of Victory, Bread of Life, Honey Drops, Ethnic Voice, Global Peace, Christian Life and Leadership Now. He has participated events in UN and Parliament of World Religions. Since 1986 he is teaching in several bible schools as guest lecturer.

He raised funds for the purpose of mission and supported several projects and to name a few are Christian Life Service, SAIACS (Southern Asia Institute of Advanced Christian Studies), Peace Store a Thrift Shop, Tsunami Projects, Hunsur CSI Church, Karnataka Missions Network and Rayagiri CSI School. Currently Paul is the CEO of InterChristian Initiatives based in Princeton, NJ, USA. InterChristian International is involved in educating, empowering and engaging Churches and Missions around the World by establishing InterChristian Institutes, Senate, Networks, Councils, Schools, Hospitals, Services, Projects and Enterprises. He is personally engaged in preaching, teaching and counseling. His teaching specialty topics include Christian Life, Death & Dying, Evangelism & Discipleship, Church Growth & Congregational Development, Leadership Development, Pastoral Studies, Pastoral Theology, Christian Ministry, Missions & Management, Relationship Enrichment, Domestic Violence, and Clinical Pastoral Education. He accepts invitation to be with your group and or congregation. Please contact him via email ceo@interchristian.org

We need more people to join the Dream Team as Volunteer, Coordinators to run various program and projects in their own counties to countries. As Paul believes that life is once and soon be passed, whatever we do for God and the Kingdom of God will stay forever. He invites everyone to join him to be involved in doing whatever we can, from wherever we are, and with whatever we have and make a difference in the life of people in need of care, peace and life eternal.
