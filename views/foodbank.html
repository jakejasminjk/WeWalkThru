InterChristian FoodBank

InterChristian FoodBank

The founder and the CEO of the mission lost his Mother at the age of seven and depended on his relatives and church community for all his basic needs. He studied in government public school and in Seminary on scholarship. During his Christian ministry of 30 years, he visited several villages and sensed a strong need to raise support for the Kids living without basic needs. Now that he has established InterChristian International, he envisions to help the families in need of food and basic needs.

We invite every church and or every individual family to support a child on a regular basis. If you are interested in donating non perishable food, please contact the office of InterChristian Initiatives with their toll free number.

We involved in With your monthly support of a dollar a day, the child will get their basic needs such as clean water, good health, food, clothing and education met who otherwise will have no way of surviving. You support will elevate the children from their poverty and give them a gift of life with all these basic needs met.

The above photos shows that we have involved in generating food and other medical stuff and send it to the Tsunami Victims in India and Sri Lanka.

If you would like to coordinate to collect nonperishable food items please contact us. There are always migrant communities and local residents in need of food.

Please contact the office of the InterChristian International in Princeton, New Jersey, USA for more details of our projects and programs.
