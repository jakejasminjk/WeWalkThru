InterChristian Institute for Ministerial Leadership

InterChristian International offers an unique opportunity to every Church and Mission Institutions to initiate InterChristian Institute for Theological Higher Education in their own settings. We offer help in establishing syllabus, and train teachers and students. Programs could be from certificate level to Doctoral level in Christian Ministry. InterChristian International Senate accredits all these program. We invite the like minded pastors and ministers around the world to join us in this venture of producing more harvesters who will become Pastors, Pastoral Educators, and Missionaries in various settings.

The founder of the mission is a Clinical Pastoral Educator and a Pastoral Clinician and involved in various teaching aspects of the ministries for the past thirty years. Once we receive a request to run a seminar or Institute in your group or church we would identify an expert in the field of your request and depute to run the course in your vicinity. Please contact the office of the InterChristian International. Below given are dozen of Institutes we run in various settings on national and international stage. We have tried to explain about at-least couple of institutes in details. Several InterChristian Institutes are organized for the benefits of people of faith. They include in the subjects of Chaplaincy & Clinical Pastoral Education, Church & Mission Management, Congregational Development, Evangelism & Church Growth, Leadership Development, Relationship Enrichment, Small Group Ministry, Sunday School Ministry, Theological Higher Education, Youth Ministry, Urban Ministry, Mission & Ministries and many more.

Course Duration: Courses are conducted for 40 Hours, 2400 Minutes. It could be organized on different schedule. The schedule timings are arranged according to season.

Course Fee: InterChristian International raises funds to run this course free. However, we encourage the donation to remunerate the faculty and self-support the program. The cost to run each institute depends on various factors including number of students. The more number attends the institute is better to keep the cost down.

InterChristian International offers an unique opportunity to every Church and Mission Institutions to initiate InterChristian Institute for Leadership Development who will become second line leader and eventually the successor. This is an initiative to develop skilled leaders for the future, who will make a difference in their leadership style and quality in exercising their leadership in Churches, NGOs and Missional and Educational Institutions. We do not train bosses but passionate leaders. Our Leadership program is designed for success based on clinical pastoral education. We help the leaders to discover and develop emotional intelligence. We invite Churches and Missions to partner with us to run this School in your settings.

InterChristian Institute for Chaplaincy and Clinic Pastoral Education

InterChristian International offers an unique opportunity to every Church and Mission Institutions to initiate InterChristian Academy for Chaplaincy and Clinical Pastoral Education. We help the churches and Missions to develop and run Chaplaincy courses based on Clinical Pastoral Education and action reflection model. The trained Chaplains offer emotional and spiritual support.to the sick and their beloved ones at the hospital, nursing homes, rehabilitation centers, correctional facilities, and cancer center. Chaplains also offer hospice care to the terminally ill and their family members. Now the chaplaincy is growing in various other fields such as educational institutions, and industries. This program will help the congregation to develop Support Groups to help those who have experienced losses and help them to cope with the grief and loss. Chaplains are trained to offer palliative care for those who are suffering with chronic illness to live their life in fullness even while they are on treatment. This program will help the Churches and Mission to enhance the spiritual healing ministry. We invite Churches and Missions to partner with us to run this Academy in your settings.

InterChristian Senate for Theological Higher Education

InterChristian International has created a Senate for theological higher education with highly qualified theologians and professors. The senate intends to empower the grass root level church community and small institutions to train more pastors, pastoral educators, and missionaries. InterChristian initiatives invite application from Bible Colleges, Seminaries, Bible Institutes, and Counseling Centers for affiliation and accreditation. Please write to ceo@interchristian.org for more details.

Please contact the office of the InterChristian International in Princeton, New Jersey, USA for more details of our projects and programs.
