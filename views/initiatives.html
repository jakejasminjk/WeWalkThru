InterChristian Service

InterChristian Service is the main mission of the orgnaization InterChristian Initiatives. InterChristian International initiates several worldwide service program and projects. We train and position personals who would involve in serving their own community holistically. Implementing various Social Care Projects, establishing educational Institutions, financial empowerment projects and health care services are the plan of action which would ensure a lifetime of smiles in the life of those in need of care.

We are seeking to establish and implement program that will cater to the felt needs of the downtrodden.
