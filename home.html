Initiatives International

A Membership Movement United to Serve

InterChristian Initiatives International is a worldwide membership movement that connects Christian believers from around the world to serve the world in unity and fulfill the great commission of the Lord Jesus Christ as one body. InterChristian International initiates several programs and projects for its members to fulfill the vision of the Lord Jesus Christ. Membership is open for individuals to Institutions irrespective of denominations. The mission of InterChristian International is to help the Believers, Churches, Ministry Professionals, Missional Institutions, Seminaries and Bible Colleges, and NGOs of healthcare, social, justice, and educational sectors to envision and network.

InterChristian Society

A Mandate

InterChristian International initiates several programs and projects in order to fulfill the vision of bringing the believers to live a life in fullness and in one accord. Church is a ministry and Institution. We are invited to experience the divine love, peace and joy in celebration of the togetherness as Believers. The InterChristian Society is a ministry to help the believers to envision the relationship, resources, responsibility and the rewards promised at the end of our life.

InterChrist Seminar

A Ministry

InterChristian International initiates several Educational programs beginning from InterChristian Seminars to InterChristian Seminary to teach the vision of the mission of the Kingdom of God as per the great commission. Education is the key to have a vision and then fulfill it as a mission with our life experiences. We are mandated to go and preach, teach and make disciple for the purpose of the Kingdom.

InterChristian Service

A Mission

InterChristian International initiates several worldwide service program and projects. We train and position personals who would involve in serving their own community holistically. Implementing various Social Care Projects, establishing educational Institutions, financial empowerment projects and health care services are the plan of action which would ensure a lifetime of smiles in the life of those in need of care.
